Help and user manual

Thank you for using Guardianes 3.0 (preview). Guardianes 3.0 is still under development, it uses the latest web technology for a enhanced experience. This is a new version and your help and feedback can really help us improve.

The goal of Guardinanes is to monitor the forest. Areas where no deforestation should occur have been selected  and split into hexagons. we need your help to monitor this hexagons and report if the forest is ok, or if deforestation is taking place.

Here is how:

0. If this is your first visit, you are "anonymous", you can make your contribution under your name or an alias by changing your name on the top right corner:

1. As you enter the app, a random hexagon is assigned for you to review. That is the darker hexagon in the middle of the screen:

 

 2. To understand if deforestation has occured, you can move back in time by sliding the time slider. 

 

3. If you do not observe differences between the last 2 dates, then you can click "all is ok!".

 

That particular hexagon is marked green and you can move to the next hexagon.

4. To move to the next hexagon, you can click on one of the little arrows in the neighbouring hexagons.



5. if you do see a difference between 2013 and 2015, you can report on the difference by telling us what you suspect. Click on the Road button ( ) if you see a road being cut through the forest or click on the deforestation button () if you see the forest disappear but are unsure why. As we know more about the area, more deforestation categories will be added.

6. After clicking on the deforestation button you can click inside the hexagon where you see the deforestation happening. That hexagon will be marked (with exact location of the problem). when you finish inspecting the hexagon you can move to a new one (step 4).

NB: You should only report new deforestation (changes between 2013 and 2015). Roads or missing forest already in the 2013 images should not be reported.


7. At any time you can zoom out or in in order to observe the "big picture" of the area or a detail inside a hexagon via the zoom control or mouse wheel.



8. You can delete deforestation observations by clicking on them and selecting delete.

9. Once you have contributed, you can simply close the webbrowser and your contribution is logged. you can return anytime to contribute more. The contributions of the users can be reviewd in statistics link (upper right corner).
